ABOUT ME


Update: I am looking for a summer internship

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Technion, Israel, under the supervision of Prof. Yoav Schechner. My research explores the interface between machine learning, physics-based imaging, 3D tomography, and computational photography. I obtained my B.Sc. in 2019 and an M.Sc. in 2021, both at the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Technion, Israel. In 2021 I conducted a summer internship at Amazon Web Service - AI labs. During my internship, our paper "GLASS: Global to Local Attention for Scene-Text Spotting" was accepted to ECCV'22, as an oral presentation. Additionally, my paper "Plankton Reconstruction through Robust Statistical Optical Tomography" won the JOSA A "Emerging Researcher Best Paper Prize" for 2021.
